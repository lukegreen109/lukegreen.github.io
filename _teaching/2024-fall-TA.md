Course Description
======
Differential and integral calculus: limits; continuity; the derivative and applications; extrema; the definite integral; fundamental theorem of calculus; L'Hopital's rule.

Students
======
68 students, 2 sections

Responsibilities
======
- Taught 2 hours of lecture 2 days per week (4 hours per week) for 16 weeks
- Graded all exams
- 3 Hours of office hours per week
