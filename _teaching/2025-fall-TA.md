Course Description
======
A treatment of algorithms used to solve problems. Topics include complexity and data, approximation theory, recursive algorithms, linear optimization, unconstrained optimization, constrained optimization, global optimization, probability theory, importance sampling, fourier series.

Students
======
49 students, 2 sections

Responsibilities
======
- Graded all rigorous proofs for 3 written assignments per week
- Setup Canvas and Gradescope course with autograders
- 3 Hours of office hours per week
